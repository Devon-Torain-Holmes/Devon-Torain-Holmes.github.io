UAP Solution

The best way I found to solve any IT Network problem was with the UAP method, which stands for Understand, Apply and Practice.

This basis method helped me solve many network issues and network assignment questions.It works by breaking down the problem or issue into stages of understanding and practical viewpionts, to help you to recognize the root problem.

Lets see how it works.



Acronym Meaning Action
U Understanding.

First understand the topic in depth. Go to the internet and learn as much as you can on the topic, on the question being asked or IT problem at hand. You should understand it so much so that you can explain it to a child. (Fineman technique).

Once you understand it to that level add more detail. Study it in depth by learning how it works, and it does not work. 		Question the Question or problem?

Find out the background of the question.

Get a understanding of the systems?

How to: Use resource like Textbooks, You Tube and the internet to gain this understanding.
A Apply your understanding.

After you have gained the knowledge of how the topic works take that understanding and apply it to the question to see if you have the answers to the problem.

See how the knowledge gain can help solve the problem and see if the answer is related to the question. 		How to: Take the understanding and apply it to the question and see how it brings a solution.
P Practice.

If it is a practical question, you can increase your understanding by practicing the configuration on Hyper V. This help you put into practice of what you have learned. 		How to: Practice the solution via Hyper V or any virtual environment.


Super Tip Do not forget to document after each install, configuration or deployments.

To take note of the where you found the answers,the page number of the book, the video or the link to the site you got the solution from.

Rate yourself on how you did, by looking at on how you could have improved or be more efficiency in your overall performance.
